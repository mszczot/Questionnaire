Thank you for completing our questionnaire.

This study aims to find a relationship between vegetarianism, executive functions, and healthy choices. The three tasks you completed were:
  • the Stroop task - which tests for inhibitory control,
  • the N-back task – which tests for working memory, and
  • a Cognitive Task-Switching task – which tests for cognitive flexibility

Inhibitory control, working memory, and cognitive flexibility are core executive functions. Executive functions are important in every aspect of life. Studies have shown executive functions have an impact on mental health, physical health, quality of life, job/school success, and marital harmony (Diamond, 2013).

This study hypothesises that vegetarians have better executive functions and make healthier choices.
